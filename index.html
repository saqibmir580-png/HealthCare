Our journey

Govt BHSS Sanzipora Mawer is a well-known educational institution recognized for providing excellent education and fostering the overall development of students. Under the leadership of our principal,Mr. Mohammad Shafi Wani, and the guidance of dedicated teachers like Er. Hilal Ahmad Shah, our school not only offers quality education but also encourages participation in various creative activities. This nurturing environment allowed me, Suhana Rashid, to develop a Digital Health Tracking Application. I would like to extend special thanks to the INSPIRE Award(‘Innovation in Science Pursuit for Inspired Research’ (INSPIRE) scheme), a flagship program of the Department of Science & Technology (DST), Government of India. The INSPIRE - MANAK (Million Minds Augmenting National Aspirations and Knowledge) initiative, executed by DST in collaboration with the National Innovation Foundation – India (NIF), aims to inspire students aged 10-15, studying in classes 6 to 10, to innovate and think creatively. Additionally, I am grateful for the support from our district CEO, Mr. Ab Majeed Dar, which has been instrumental in fostering my creativity and encouraging me to bring my ideas to life.
